### Project: **"Botany Bud" Website**

**Purpose:**

* Help people discover and learn about plants that suit their living spaces and lifestyles.
* Educate about plant care and benefits.

**Audience:**

* Plant enthusiasts
* Beginners looking for easy-to-care-for plants
* People decorating homes or offices

**Goal:**

* Assist users in finding plants that fit their environment and preferences.
* Provide basic plant care tips.
* Make learning about plants fun + interactive.

---

### Pages & Ideas

1. **Home Page:**

   * Welcome message: “Find the perfect plant for your space!”
   * Quick links to: “Browse Plants”, “About Us”, “Contact” etc.
   * “Plant of the Week” with click to reveal fun fact

2. **Discover Plants Page:**

   * List of plants, could be categorized by:

     * **Light needs:** Low, Medium, High
     * **Care difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
     * **Environment:** Indoor, Outdoor
     * JS: small interactive quiz (“Which plant fits your home?”) where the user answers 2–3 questions and gets a suggestion

3. **About Page:**

   * Short story about why you created the site
   * “Meet the Team” section with fake avatars

4. **Contact Page:**

   * Email form (name, email, message)
   * Social media links (Instagram, etc.)
   * Maybe small “suggest a plant” feature

5. **Other**

   * Light/dark mode toggle?
   * Click-to-reveal plant care tips

---

**Specific:** 4-page website to help people look for plants

**Measurable:** 1 page a day OR all html in 2 days + all css in 2 days + javascript in 1 day

**Achievable:** To keep simple, leave aesthetics for last, focus on html and then css worry about javascript last
